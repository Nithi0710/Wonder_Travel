Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trip?

Booking a trip with WonderTravel is easy! First, browse our destinations and select the one you're interested in. Click the "Book Now" button, and you'll be guided through our simple booking process:

  1. Select the number of adults traveling
  2. Choose your journey dates
  3. Enter traveler details
  4. Provide contact information
  5. Complete the payment

After successful payment, you'll receive a confirmation email with all your booking details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods to make your booking convenient:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Net Banking
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.)
  • Wallet Payments (Paytm, Amazon Pay)

All payments are secured using industry-standard encryption to ensure your financial information is protected.

Can I modify or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking, subject to our cancellation policy:

  • Cancellations made 30+ days before departure: 90% refund
  • Cancellations made 15-29 days before departure: 50% refund
  • Cancellations made 7-14 days before departure: 25% refund
  • Cancellations made less than 7 days before departure: No refund

To modify or cancel your booking, please contact our customer support team with your booking reference number.

What's included in the package price?

Our standard packages typically include:

  • Accommodation in carefully selected hotels
  • Daily breakfast and select meals as specified in the itinerary
  • Airport transfers
  • Guided sightseeing tours as mentioned in the itinerary
  • All applicable taxes

Please note that airfare is generally not included unless specifically mentioned. Optional activities and personal expenses are also not included in the package price.

Do I need travel insurance?

While travel insurance is not mandatory for booking with us, we strongly recommend it for all travelers. Travel insurance provides coverage for unexpected events such as:

  • Trip cancellations or interruptions
  • Medical emergencies during your trip
  • Lost or delayed baggage
  • Travel delays

We can recommend reliable travel insurance providers or you can arrange your own coverage.

What documents do I need for international travel?

For international travel, you typically need:

  • A valid passport with at least 6 months validity from your return date
  • Visa for the destination country (requirements vary by nationality)
  • Travel insurance documentation
  • Confirmed return/onward tickets
  • Sufficient funds for your stay

Specific requirements may vary by destination. We provide detailed information about document requirements during the booking process for your specific destination.

Can I book for a large group?

Yes, we specialize in both individual and group bookings. For groups larger than 10 people, we recommend contacting our customer support directly to discuss customized options and potential group discounts.

We can arrange specialized itineraries for:

  • Family reunions
  • Corporate retreats
  • School/college trips
  • Wedding parties
  • Friend groups
